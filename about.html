About O4B
Mission
Out for Business at Berkeley is committed to fostering diversity, inclusivity, and a strong community for LGBTQIA undergraduates interested in the fields of business, consulting, banking, marketing, management, and similar industries through developing, inspiring, and connecting the next generation of professionals.
About
We strive to create an encompassing professional and social environment for our members in which they can discover their professional passions, succeed in these pursuits, and be supported in their identity and diversity.
We do this by: partnering and engaging with various corporate partners across many industries, hosting anything from case workshops, info-sessions, to coffee chats; creating MBA-undergrad mentorship platforms; and much more.
